Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist and former chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, one of India's largest and oldest conglomerates. He was born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India. Ratan Tata is known for his significant contributions to the growth and diversification of the Tata Group during his tenure as chairman.
